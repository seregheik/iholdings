PrimeHoldings

PrimeHoldings works like a personal investment manager and adviser that lives on your smartphone/computer, helping you invest in dollar denominated assets carefully selected to deliver safe, consistent dollar returns over time.

PrimeHoldings is the only platform that allows you to allocate capital across a portfolio of stocks, rental properties, cryptocurrency and global fixed income assets, all in dollars. We vet the assets for quality, long term potential and their capacity to deliver stable returns for you in all market conditions

You can start investing from just $2,000 and there is no maximum. PrimeHoldings can invest for you whether you’re just starting your journey or you have millions in capital to preserve.

You can withdraw on maturity, and the shortest duration is 30 Days. All withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours.

PrimeHoldings is a New York trust company regulated by the New York State Department of Financial Services (NYSDFS)

PrimeHoldings is a digital wealth manager, we provide managed portfolios selected and managed through our app. Users cannot trade or pick securities by themselves.

Two-factor authentication (2FA), also known as 2-step verification, is a security layer in addition to your username and password. With 2FA enabled on your account, you will have to provide your password (first "factor") and your 2FA code (second "factor") when signing in to your account. 2FA codes are associated with a specific device (such as your phone) or email

You can withdraw as many times as possible, there are no penalties.

Interests are added every day based on the Roi on the plan.
